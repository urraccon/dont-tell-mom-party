djs

  • Radox

    #techno

    “I really wanna see you dance on the hard beats and I really wanna see you move on the high BPM!”🎊🎉 Radu Constantin, known as Radox , is a Romanian artist who recently emerged in Bucharest's underground music scene. Radox is renowned for his fusion of Hard Techno and PSY, his music effortlessly blending the trippy psychedelic elements with infectious groovy techno rhythms, all underpinned by the punch of powerful pop beats, subtly flavoured with industrial influences. Having performed at renowned venues like Katakomba and Nether, Radox is now gearing up for upcoming appearances on podcasts in both Romania and Germany.

  • Madame 8

    #techno

    EN: Madame 8 is a nonbinary music selector passionate by culture, studying disco and balroom with the help of their mothers for 4 years now. Their eclectic sound, influenced directly by the dance styles they fathom is both an invitation to dance adressed towards the local nighltife scene as well as a political statement, curating sets of house, disco and vogue beats for the floor. RO: Madame 8 este selectoare de muzica nonbinara pasionata de cultura, invatand despre disco si ballroom cu ajutorul mamelor sale de 4 ani incoace. Sunetul sau eclectic, influentat direct de catre stilurile de dans pe care le aprofundeaza sunt atat o invitatie la dans adresata catre scena de nightlife locala cat si o afirmatie politica, organizand seturi de house, disco si vogue beats pentru ringul de dans.

  • DJ Hernandez

    #techno

    DJ Hernandez's music styles vary from Tribal House to Chicago House to Deep, Garage, and even European Progressive, but his signature style is now Berlin Techno. Since his relocation back to Romania, DJ Hernandez put his music hobby on hold until he was offered the opportunity to hold a residency at the best Techno Party in Romania which is Don't Tell Mom Party, a monthly party where every member can feel safe, understood and taken cared for. Stilurile muzicale ale DJ Hernandez variază de la Tribal House la Chicago House la Deep, Garage și chiar European Progressive, dar stilul său semnătură este acum Berlin Techno. De la relocarea sa inapoi in Romania, DJ Hernandez si-a pus hobby-ul muzical în așteptare până i s-a oferit posibilitatea de a ține un rezidențiat la cea mai buna petrecere Techno din Romania, care este Don't Tell Mom Party, o petrecere lunară în care fiecare membru se poate simți în siguranță.
